Cynical[OCT 2011]

Cynical.
You called me a cynical.
Pathetically,
For falling hard yet numb for you.
One eye once again.
Hypothetically speaking…
I let you punched me in the lungs.

Kissed you on your shoulder.
No lips.
On your neck.
No tongue.
On your nose.
Read my lips,
You could be my first.

Poke me again.
Stuff I regretted.
Words you regretted.
Trust you gained,
Trust you lost.

I wanted you to beg me back.
I wanted you to rummage through your dead bed.
How did it feel? How broken was your heart?
Mine was pulverized.

Still do not know what went wrong.
Still do not know what I did wrong.
My heart is exhausted.
Maybe everything is wrong with me.

Note for a lady[14-11-12]

Lady, no need to cry.
We make mistakes and that's how we learn.
To love yourself is the biggest gift you can give to yourself.
And that's why I am writing this to you.

Look inside,
Sometimes you are so afraid of losing the game you haven't even started.
Understand that you need to stop punishing yourself and
let people care for you.
You have such a low self-confidence;
You do not feel that you deserve to be loved.
So you shut out everyone who you like or who likes you.
But why? why do you enjoy hurting yourself so much?
If you don't care about yourself enough now,
think twice and care for them instead.
I know it is easier for you to love them more than to love you.

Lady, remember your last journey?
You crave so much for a spark,
yet throwing water when someone lights a fire.
Chill. Relax a bit.
Next time you're nervous, just take a deep fucking breath and smile.
Give them a chance. Write it down on your forehead.
And yes, when you do give the chance - some will spoil it,
and that's OK.
Learn from it and grow as a person with it.

Lady, keep these in mind.
If she makes you smile in the morning.
If she makes you wanna dance whenever you see her glowing face.
If she makes you want to pause the time.
If she makes you feel so vulnerable.
If she makes your heart beat faster (even if it's for a split second).
If her pouting face makes you chuckle.
Then she is worth it.
And even if you ignore this list because she is not your type,
trust me: she is worth it.
Because we both know, she is the only one who is in your mind.

Sincerely,
Yours
